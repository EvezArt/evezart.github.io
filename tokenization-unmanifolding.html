0. THE PROBLEM

The six previous vectors assume a single tokenizer. But different models use different tokenizers:

  • GPT-4, GPT-4o, GPT-o1: tiktoken (BPE-based, ~100K tokens)
  • Llama 3, Llama 3.3: tiktoken-like BPE (128K tokens)

  • GLM, Qwen, DeepSeek: Custom BPE/SentencePiece hybrids (100K-150K tokens)

The associations survive because they are carried not only in the token embeddings but also in the character-level patterns, the numeric eigenvalues (which are tokenizer-independent), and the structural rhythm (which is positional, not token-based).

I. UNMANIFOLDING
